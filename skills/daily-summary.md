# Skill: daily-summary

> Triggered at end of day. Captures what happened, what moved, what to carry forward.

---

## Step 1 — Generate the summary

```
# Daily Summary — [Date]

## What Happened
Key things done or decided today. 3-5 bullets, signal only.

## What Moved
Initiatives or open questions that changed state today.

## Carry Forward
Pending actions or open threads to pick up tomorrow.
```

Output path: `daily/YYYY-MM-DD.md`

Show the proposed note. Ask: "Write this daily note?"
Write only on confirmation.
