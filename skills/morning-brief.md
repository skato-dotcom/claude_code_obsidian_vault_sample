# Skill: morning-brief

> Triggered at the start of the day. Surfaces what matters today.

---

## Step 1 — Load context

Read SESSION-HANDOVER.md for pending actions carried from the last session.
Check `daily/` for yesterday's note if it exists.

---

## Step 2 — Generate the brief

```
# Morning Brief — [Date]

## Carried Forward
Open actions from last session that are still pending.

## Today's Focus
1-3 things that matter most today, based on pending actions and open questions.

## Watch For
Anything time-sensitive: decisions due, meetings to prep for, asks that need a response.
```

Output directly. No staging needed.
